1. About

I am a PhD student in the School of Computer and Communication Sciences at EPFL, advised by Prof. Pascal Frossard in the Signal Processing Laboratory 4 (LTS4). My research focuses on AI safety, model editing, and multimodal learning.

Before joining EPFL, I obtained my Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Science from the American University of Beirut (AUB), during which I interned at MIT's MadryLab.
